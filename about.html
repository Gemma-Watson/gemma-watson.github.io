About Me

Hi, I'm Gemma Watson, a pre-service teacher studying at Australian Catholic University.

What I'm Studying

I am completing a Bachelor of Education (Secondary) and Bachelor of Arts (Design and Innovation Technologies), specialising in Textiles, Design and Technology, Food Technology, and Computing.

What Drives Me

I have always loved working with my hands, designing creatively, and exploring how tech and design connect. I bring discipline, creativity, and resilience into my classroom.

My Teaching Philosophy

I am passionate about building safe, supportive classrooms where every student feels seen, challenged, and inspired. My teaching blends:

  • Project-based learning
  • Design thinking
  • Hands-on activities
  • Real-world applications

I aim to make learning accessible for diverse learners, including EAL/D students and those needing extra support. I want students to leave my class feeling confident in what they can do, and who they are becoming.
