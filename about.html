My Background

My name is Gemma Watson and I am currently studying a Bachelor of Education (Secondary) and a Bachelor of Design and Innovation Technologies, specialising in Textiles, Food Technology, and Computing, which are part of the NESA curriculum, Nesa Syllabus. I have always loved working with my hands, creating unique designs, and exploring how technology can be used creatively. My background as a high-level gymnast taught me discipline, focus, and resilience, which I now bring into the classroom. This experience shaped who I am and sparked my interest in helping others discover their own passions through learning. I believe education should be engaging, empowering, and inclusive for every student.

As a pre-service teacher, I am passionate about creating safe, supportive spaces where students feel seen, challenged, and inspired. My lessons are interactive and hands-on, often blending design thinking, real-world contexts, and opportunities for creativity. I especially enjoy teaching in project-based subjects like Design and Technology, where students can see their ideas come to life. I'm also committed to making learning accessible for diverse learners, including those who are EAL/D or need additional support. I want students to leave my classroom feeling capable and confident, not just in what they know, but in who they are becoming.
