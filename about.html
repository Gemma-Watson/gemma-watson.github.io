ABOUT

Hi, I'm Gemma Watson

Secondary education and design student who values effort, humour, and clear purpose in everything I do.

About Me

I’m training to teach textiles, design and technology, food technology, and computing. I grew up in North Turramurra and have always been a practical, independent person. I used to be an elite gymnast, which shaped how I work — disciplined, focused, and never halfway with effort. Those habits carried into how I study, design, and teach.

How I Teach

I like teaching with humour and energy. I want students to actually enjoy being in class, not just get through it. I focus on making lessons clear, purposeful, and hands-on so they see progress and feel proud of what they’ve learned.

The best moments for me are when something clicks for a student — when they go from confused to confident. I like giving them enough structure to feel secure but enough freedom to try, fail, and still want to keep going.

What I Believe

I think school should be a place students look forward to, not somewhere they dread. My goal is to build a space that’s calm, fair, and safe — where expectations are high, but students know I’m on their side.

I don’t want to be the teacher who’s unapproachable or dismissive. I want to be consistent, honest, and human. I believe in effort, in doing things properly, and in helping students take pride in their work.
